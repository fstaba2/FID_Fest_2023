DTFID Fest 2023

The second annual Fall FID Fest will be held at Princeton Campus on October 3rd. It is a full-day internal festival that will feature a range of talks covering the latest projects of DT Financial Instruments Datasets (DTFID) teams, guest speakers, handing out awards, and fun activities.
You are all invited to participate in-person in Princeton, or virtually on Zoom. The event will be held in the Princeton Building 100 Auditorium for the day and the Princeton Building 100 Back Patio for the evening. The nominees for awards will be introduced by team leads. Stay tuned for more information.
